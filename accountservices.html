Outsourcing of Accounts

When setting up of a new business, in the rush to become operational, accounting function is very often overlooked until some considerable time which can cause serious problems.

For many small and medium sized companies, hiring a full time accountant is not only expensive but also unnecessary, given the relatively small volume of accountancy work required. However, neglect of the accounting function can prove to be very costly.

Outsourcing also eliminates the interruption caused by accounts staff going on leave or during times of heavy work pressure such as year end reporting. We provide a professional and reliable accounting and financial reporting service using up to date accounting software.

Accounting services

We provide efficient and cost effective accounting solutions that are powered by latest accounting tools and software.
• Setting up of accounting procedures
• Identifying accounting software & it’s implementation
• Part time & periodic bookkeeping services by professionals
• Periodic financial reporting as per IAS/ IFRS
• Supervision & review of accounting
• Forecasts, Budgets and Projections
• Train & supervise accountant

Audit services

With the in-depth knowledge and technical expertise we have gained over the years, we can provide a professional approach towards internal audit that is capable of yielding immense opportunities for the business.

• Ensuring whether the required internal controls are in place.
• Review and evaluate existing internal controls, identifying faults and outlining methods for improvement
• Analyze, in depth, the controls and accounting policies
• Regular reports on on-going performance of the company
• Verify transactions and report compliance with respect to the companies' policies & procedures
• Ensure early detection of fraud and mitigate its effect on the business
